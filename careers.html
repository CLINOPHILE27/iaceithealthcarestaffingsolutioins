Registered Nurse

Location: Onsite

We are looking for a skilled nurse provide high-quality care to patients in a variety of settings, such as hospitals, clinics, and private homes.

Requirements:
- With current CRNM registration in Manitoba
- At least 1 year of nursing experience (any field of expertise).
- Has/ had work with senior clients/ residents (an asset).
- Possesses excellent work ethics and must be in good health to do the work.
- Immunization Record, Covid Immunization (at least 2 doses).
- N95 Mask Fit testing certificate.
- BLS certificate/ ACLS and other certificates available.
- Winnipeg Criminal Record Check and other Vulnerability Sector checks.
- Valid Driver’s license (preferred).
- Can travel within Winnipeg and Rural Manitoba.

Disability Worker/ Personal Support Worker

Location: Onsite

A disability worker or personal support worker helps people with disabilities live independently and reach their potential.

Requirements:
- With current training/s from a recognized College/ Institute (preferred).
- At least 3 months working experience (health assistant/companion/PCH).
- Has/ had work with senior clients/ residents (an asset).
- Possesses excellent work ethics and must be in good health to do the work.
- Immunization Record, Covid Immunization (at least 2 doses).
- N95 Mask Fit testing certificate (preferred but not required).
- BLS certificate/ First Aid, other certificates available.
- Winnipeg Criminal Record Check and other Vulnerability Sector checks.
- Valid Driver’s license (preferred).
- Must be outgoing and friendly to our seniors and their families.
- With good communication skills.
- Able to assit / perform basic Activity of Daily living ( ADLS) such as cooking, meal preparation, bathing, running errands, shopping & light housekeeping.
- Can work under minimal supervision.
